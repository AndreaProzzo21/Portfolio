What about me?

I am currently completing my degree in Digital Automation Engineering, where I focus on bridging advanced technologies and real-world applications. I am passionate about robotics, artificial intelligence, digital twins, optimization, and data analytics. What drives me is turning complex problems into practical, efficient, and innovative solutions, blending theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience.

My goal is to contribute to projects that push technological boundaries and create meaningful impact. In this portfolio, I share my vision, interests, and selected projects that reflect my curiosity, problem-solving mindset, and commitment to continuous learning.
